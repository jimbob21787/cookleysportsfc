About Us

The U11 Hornets are a passionate and developing team within Cookley Sports FC. We focus on building confidence, skills, and sportsmanship among young players in a supportive and fun environment.

Training Times

We train every Wednesday and Friday evening at Cookley Village Hall & Sports Club from 6:00 PM to 7:30 PM. All new players are welcome!

We are proudly supported by local businesses that believe in youth football.

About the U11 Hornets

The U11 Hornets are a passionate and energetic team committed to developing their skills, teamwork, and love for football. Coached by experienced volunteers, our focus is on player growth, enjoyment, and building confidence both on and off the pitch.

Training Times

We train every Wednesday and Saturday:

  • Wednesdays: 6:00pm – 7:15pm at Cookley Village Hall Pitches
  • Saturdays: 10:00am – 11:15am at Cookley School Fields
