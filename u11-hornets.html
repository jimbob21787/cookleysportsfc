About Us

The U11 Hornets are a spirited and dedicated group of young players who bring energy, passion, and teamwork to every game. Our team focuses on player development, respect, and having fun while learning the beautiful game.

Training Times

We train every Tuesday and Thursday evening from 6:00 PM to 7:30 PM at Cookley Village Hall & Sports Club.
