Welcome to Cookley Sports FC

We are a passionate grassroots football club based in Cookley, providing football opportunities for all ages and abilities. Whether you're a young aspiring star or just love the beautiful game, Cookley Sports FC is the place to play, grow, and thrive.

About Us

Cookley Sports Football Club has been a part of the community for decades, offering football to players of all levels. With dedicated coaches and volunteers, we focus on development, teamwork, and fun.

Our Teams

We have junior and senior teams competing in local leagues. Come down and support us on match day!
